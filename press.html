Dr. Weintraub's expertise has been featured across national media including television, podcasts, and leading health platforms. Dr. Weintraub is available for expert commentary on GLP-1 receptor agonists, obesity pharmacotherapy, diabetes care, metabolic health, and clinical endocrinology.
